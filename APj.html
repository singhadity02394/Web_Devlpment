DR.A.P.J ABDUL KALAM

(1931-2015)

Missile Man Of India

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an eminent Indian scientist, politician, and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's space and missile programs and was known for his simplicity, humility, and patriotism. He served as the President from 2002 to 2007 and focused on inspiring the youth. Dr. Kalam received several awards, including the Bharat Ratna, and authored the popular autobiography "Wings of Fire." His contributions and vision continue to inspire generations.
